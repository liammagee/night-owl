Course Project
You will identify and analyze an issue of inequality as it relates to artificial intelligence in education that you are interested in exploring through the series of assignments below. You may choose a topic from the examples provided in course materials or your own example (subject to instructor approval).

Project Part 1 (35%)
 
For Part 1 of the project, you will identify a topic about inequality, AI, and Education of interest to you, investigate this topic using course materials and external resources, and develop a brief overview of the topic. Your paper should cover the theoretical context and aims of the topic using appropriate course materials and external scholarly and media resources. Part 1 should be 3-4 pages in length.

Project Part 2 (45%)
Part 2 of the project includes 1) a revised topic overview (incorporating feedback received on Part 1), and 2) an analysis of the resources used in your chosen example and more detailed examinations of possible responses to the challenge you identify. Your analysis should incorporate evidence from academic research. In total, Part 2 should be 6-8 pages in length.
